From Historic Bank to Local Coffee Gem

A treasured gathering place in the Heart of Coeur d'Alene, Vault Coffee is where community flourishes, friendships are made and exquisite coffee brews.

Coeur d'Alene's Finest Destination for Exceptional Coffee

At Vault Coffee, we take pride in being Coeur d'Alene's premier destination for coffee lovers seeking an extraordinary experience. Nestled in the heart of this charming Idaho city, our café offers a haven where passion, craftsmanship, and community converge. From the moment you step into Vault Coffee, you'll be greeted by the rich aromas of freshly brewed specialty coffee and the warm smiles of our dedicated baristas.

Our mission is simple: to serve the finest, ethically sourced coffee beans from around the world and transform them into exquisite beverages that awaken your senses. We meticulously select our beans, ensuring they are sustainably grown and harvested to preserve their distinctive flavors. Each cup of coffee we serve is a testament to our commitment to quality and excellence.

Meet our partners

We’re A locally owned and operated business in downtown Coeur d’Alene serving coffee, tea and delicious treats. Built in 1904, the EXCHANGE NATIONAL BANK of Coeur d’Alene has also served as an ice cream parlor, a pharmacy and even the town’s library (2nd floor) in the early 1900’s. We’re proud to serve the many generations that gather on this corner for coffee, conversation and to create their own memories!

Doma Coffee, Post Falls ID

Since 2000, DOMA has roasted high quality, ethically- sourced, direct-relationship coffees. As leaders in the Fair Trade movement, they believe their business should be a positive force in both the local and global economies. These guys and gals know their coffee.

Elmwood Inn Fine Teas, Danville KY

Southern folks really know their stuff when it comes to tea. We carry over 20 of their varieties including ceremonial grade matcha. This isn't your grandmother's tea (but we bet she'll love it too.)
